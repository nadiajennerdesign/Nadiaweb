Department of Transformation

The Department of Transformation (D.O.T.) is a framework for community engagement that our team proposed to the Wellington City Council in response to this. It utilises tactical urbanism to grant agency to citizens and facilitate meaningful community interactions in public spaces.

Bus stops are areas where the hustle of the city is suspended, even if only for a few minutes, providing the perfect opportunity for physical onboarding.

The process of this project involved many mini-presentations or 'check-ins'. Pictured are some notes preparing for one of these.

One of my favourite ideation exercises is '20 in 20'. In this you go down a list of 20 words/prompts, spending a minute on each. We used this exercise to hone in on our concept. The prompt was 'Disneyland', which got us talking about how the company customises the space of Disneyland. Are the streets of Wellington a private space, or is it open to customisation by its inhabitants?
