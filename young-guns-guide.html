The Young Guns Guide to Our Place

The Young Gun’s Guide To Our Place would tour New Zealand, gathering insider knowledge from our people. This would be collated into an online road-trip catalogue. The experience is sustained by touring every two years, rather than as a one-off.

The Young Guns Tour Truck, designed to take the guide on the road, exhibiting and gathering insider knowledge of local New Zealand towns.

A summary of the problem we were aiming to solve. As the blurb says, our problem statement is a design challenge signposting the way in which we should tackle the problem. This statement would later pivot into a central proposition on how to solve this problem.
