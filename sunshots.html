Sunshots

Our team invented Sunshots: Portable sunblock pods. These turn what can sometimes feel like a chore into an exciting experience. We chemically synthesised the pods, and developed a marketing strategy to implement in summer music festivals across New Zealand.

Each Sunshot is equivalent to one teaspoon, the recommended dose for one limb.

We used business modelling techniques to determine how our project could potentially operate. The core aim of Sunshots isn't financial, so we used the social lean canvas as our template. The social lean canvas is a business canvas template with prompts that aren't as focused on generating profit.

As our concept changed, we checked in with "swot" tables, to evaluate the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and tensions (threats). By comparing these tables to each other it allowed us to identify core strengths/weaknesses etc that are consistent across multiple versions of our idea.
