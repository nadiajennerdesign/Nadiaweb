Sunshots

Our team invented Sunshots: Portable sunblock pods. These turn a mundane act into an exciting experience. We chemically synthesised the pods, and developed a marketing strategy to implement in summer music festivals across New Zealand.

Each Sunshot is equivalent to one teaspoon, the recommended dose for one limb.
