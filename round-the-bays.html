Wellington Round the Bays

Wellington Round the Bays is the largest mass participation event in the lower North Island and the third-largest in New Zealand. This iconic community event has been going strong for 45 years, and I'm proud to be a part of the 2023 team.

This stage display was used on event day to thank our sponsors and congratulate the winners.

We worked on our social media presence for six months leading up to the event, hosting giveaways, competitions, and raising awareness for our charity partners (Cancer Society, Zealandia, The Mental Health Foundation, and Rainbow Wellington).

Wellington Airport kindly offered us space through their channels – both physical and digital – to promote our iconic Wellington event.

Although participants come from all over Aotearoa, it was important to be seen in the streets where the event itself takes place. These channels include billboards, street flags, and bus backs.

Pace runners debriefing at the start line.

This year had record-breaking merchandise sales. Merchandise is an easy way to support the event and its charity partners, plus buyers get to enjoy their vibrant design and breathable fabric :)
