Bitsy Buddies!

Bitsy Buddies aims to change children's attitudes regarding fruits and vegetables through the appropriation and co-option of marketing strategies used for confectionery and fast food brands.

Bitsy Buddies: Bite Sized Books series intended to be delivered alongside the Fruit in Schools programme.

Stickers are collected on the scenes printed on the inverse of each book.

Scratch-and-sniff fruit stickers can subtly introduce taste profiles.
