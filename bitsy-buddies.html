Bitsy Buddies!

Bitsy Buddies aims to change children's attitudes regarding fruits and vegetables through the appropriation and co-option of marketing strategies used for confectionery and fast food brands.

Bitsy Buddies: Bite Sized Books series intended to be delivered alongside the Fruit in Schools programme.

Stickers are collected on the scenes printed on the inverse of each book.

Through empathy exercises based on case studies and discussion, I explored the potential wants, needs, and fears of my intended audience.

Scratch-and-sniff fruit stickers can subtly introduce taste profiles.

Seasonal over-the-counter supermarket stickers to be handed out upon purchase of produce.

Havin’ fun in Autodesk Maya.

Binding Large scale interactive activity book.

I used market research strategies to gain an overview of projects with similar aims to mine to see where my project could fit in, and which organisations it could be useful to get in contact with.
