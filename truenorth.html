True North

True North is a careers advisory system that our team developed to empower year 10 students with confidence and awareness of career pathways True North would connect Career Ambassadors with students on the brink of deciding their main high school trajectory. “Young people who experience just four or more interactions with employers are five times less likely to be unemployed.” ~ Education and Employers Taskforce, United Kingdom.

The Ambassadors Toolbook contains guidelines for hosting the workshops, core values of True North, and an in-depth schedule outlining the week ahead.
